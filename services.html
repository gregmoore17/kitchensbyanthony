Services

Deciphering the code... Kitchens

Here at Kitchens by Anthony, we understand that Customers will have varied tastes in style. Using the consultation to its full potential will allow Anthony to create a style to best suit you, whether it is a traditional or modern design that you are searching for. In partnership with our German manufacturer Hacker, we offer a broad range of designer kitchen styles.

There is something for every taste from modern and sleek to traditionally painted doors, all of the highest specification. We pride ourselves in designing kitchens with the highest levels of engineering possible, giving many years of satisfaction as well as creating the perfect environment within the heart of the home.

Style and substance... Planning

Here at Kitchens by Anthony, the Customer comes first and it is crucial that we ensure your journey is a satisfying one and is suited to your lifestyle. We are extremely passionate about achieving your vision and invite you into our showroom to understand our range, quality and selection of wonderful German kitchens for you to choose from. We would be delighted to show you our capabilities and to assist you in making your important decisions.

We encourage you to get a feel of how we work as we believe that here at Kitchens by Anthony, we have something for everyone and understand that style, functionality as well as budget are all important factors. The Kitchen is the heart of the home and so by beginning the journey with listening to what it is that you are aiming to achieve, will set the scene. You are then offered a home visit to ensure that the end result will be that perfect fit, design and feel to suit your lifestyle.

Using the Most up to date 3D planning systems we will work with you to create images to help visualise the intended outcome of your Kitchen design. Our large planning screen will take you on a visual journey through all stages of your project, from early design to finding colours and specification. This will enable you to fully appreciate the finer details of your creation.
